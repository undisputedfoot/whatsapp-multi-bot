# 🚀 HOW TO START THE BOT
### (You can do this — it's just 2 clicks!)

---

## FIRST TIME ONLY
### Double-click: `setup.bat`
A black window opens. Wait 2-3 minutes. It closes by itself when done.

---

## EVERY TIME YOU WANT THE BOT
### Double-click: `start_bot.bat`
A browser opens automatically. **Do not close the black window!**

---

## CONNECT YOUR WHATSAPP

1. Browser opens to a login page
   - Username: `admin`
   - Password: `admin123`

2. Click the **"main"** card

3. A **QR code** appears on screen

4. On your phone:
   - Open **WhatsApp**
   - Tap ⋮ → **Linked Devices**
   - Tap **Link a Device**
   - Point camera at the QR code on your computer screen

5. ✅ Done! The bot is now connected.

---

## TRY IT OUT
Send one of these from WhatsApp to test:

| Send this | Bot replies with |
|-----------|-----------------|
| `!ping` | Pong! |
| `!help` | List of commands |
| `!weather Dubai` | Weather report |

---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT
- **Keep the black window open** — closing it stops the bot
- Your phone must have internet — WhatsApp uses your phone as a bridge
- If the bot stops responding, close the black window and double-click `start_bat.bat` again

---

## ❓ HELP! Something's not working
- **Bot won't start?** — Run `setup.bat` again
- **No QR code?** — Refresh the browser (F5 key)
- **WhatsApp disconnected?** — Scan QR code again
- **Forgot password?** — It's `admin` / `admin123`
